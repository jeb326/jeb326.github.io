About Me

John Bouchard is a semi-pro data scientist, podcast producer, and radio host with a passion for all things sports. John will graduate from the University of Pittsburgh with a Bachelors of Science in Statistics.John has work closely with WPTS radio on both his own 9am radio sports talk show,The Nine, and with other events like live broadcasts of Pitt Football Basketball, and the first ever Pitt Hockey broadcast. He also has worked on many podcast specifically the Blue Line Crew podcast which he stars and produces. John is looking to expand his skills to make hockey visualization more accessible to the general public and to listener throughout the Steel City.
